Artist By Day and Hero By night

Performing has been a passion for virtue. Singing in church since the age of 5, Virtue finds life and joy in the love of music. Her love for music grows dramitically in her mid 20's and is now faced with the truth that God may be calling her to persue her dreams as an artist. She records and writes songs that are uplifting and encouraging while keeping a catchy tune. She manages to work and save lives from 7pm-7am and after work on Sun mornings she gets prepared to get on that alter at church to sing her soul out for the lover ofher soul, Jesus Christ.
